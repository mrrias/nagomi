Pride

Celebrating LGBTQIA+ identities through education, support, visibility, and community.

Safe Space

Nagomi is a safe place for people of pride as well as allies. We believe in support, community and a loving space we can all enjoy. We look forward to hearing your story and getting to know and support each other :3

Inclusion

We believe in an inclusive space for all.
We are sure to create roles for many sexualities, genders as well as pronouns. However if we miss something please let our wonderful admins know so they can come up with a fix that helps make you feel welcomed!

Community

Nagomi is a community where people can make friends, share experiences and connect with others who understand them.
Whether you're questioning, exploring your identity or simply looking for a welcoming place to chat, you'll always find people ready to listen here.

Resources

We believe that learning helps create understanding and acceptance.
Our server provides resources, discussions and opportunities to learn about different identities, orientations, genders and members LGBTQIA+ experiences.

Support

Everyone faces challenges at times. While we are not a replacement for professional help, we strive to be a supportive environment where members can talk openly, seek advice and receive encouragement from a caring community :>
