Pride

Our goal is to create a place where YOU feel welcomed, included and safe!

Nagomi is an inclusitivity focussed community. With many server members being either apart of LGBTQIA+ or allies. We have a rich pride based community where all are welcome. Staff put time and effort into creating a safe place through moderation, communication and support for all members. Admins will always be there to listen to you and will often add new roles, channels or other features to the server when requested by members.
